DOCUMENTS FOR WELFARE CERTIFICATES

  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Residence Certificate

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • Identity Proof: Such as Aadhar Card, Voter ID, or Passport to establish the identity of the applicant.
  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent photographs of the applicant for documentation purposes.
  • Proof of Age: Documents like a birth certificate or school leaving certificate to verify the applicant's age.
  • Affidavit (if required): A sworn statement affirming the authenticity of the information provided in the application.
  • Educational Certificates: Sometimes required for specific welfare schemes related to education or scholarships.
  • Bank Account Details: To facilitate the transfer of benefits or subsidies.
