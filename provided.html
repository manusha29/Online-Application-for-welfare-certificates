PROVIDED CERTIFICATES

Identity Certificate

Identity certificates are official documents that verify an individual's identity. They typically include personal information such as the individual's name, date of birth, photograph, and a unique identification number. These certificates are essential for various legal and administrative purposes, including applying for government services, opening bank accounts, and verifying identity in various transactions.

Welfare Certificate

Welfare certificates are documents issued to individuals or families to confirm their eligibility for social welfare programs. These certificates may include information about the recipient's socio-economic status, family size, and specific welfare benefits they are entitled to receive. Welfare certificates play a crucial role in ensuring that assistance is provided to those in need, helping to improve their quality of life and access to essential services.
