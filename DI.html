DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR IDENTITY CERTIFICATES

  1. Birth Certificate
  2. Death Certificate
  3. National Identity Card

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • Proof of Address (e.g., utility bills, rental agreement, bank statement)
  • Passport-sized Photographs (recent)
  • Completed Application Form specific to the identity certificate
  • Additional Identity Proof (e.g., Driver's License, Voter ID)
  • Affidavit (if required) to affirm information authenticity
  • Educational Certificates (if related to educational identity certificates)
  • Other Supporting Documents as required by the issuing authority
